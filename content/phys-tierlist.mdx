During my honours physics degree, I took _a lot_ of courses.
I want to commemorate my time in physics in the best way possible: **a tierlist**.

Of course, every tierlist needs a rubric. Here are things I _did_ consider for each ranking:

1. _Usefulness._ How useful is the course for post-school life?
2. _Execution._ Given what the course set out to do, how well does it accomplish this?
3. _Organization._ Did the content of the course have a good logical flow? Was the scope reasonable given the time-frame?
4. _Overall vibes._ This speaks for itself.

More notably, these are things I _did not_ consider for each ranking:

1. _Professor._ Who teaches a course often changes, and it is not my intention to use this platform to provide performance reviews of faculty.
2. _Grades._ I want this tierlist to focus on the learning takeaways of each course, not how easy it is to get an A.
